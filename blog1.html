My Journey to Jupiter

It's hard to believe that just 4 days ago, I was still on Earth. Time really files when you're flying through space! I quickly found out that it gets pretty boring zipping around the universe by yourself, so I decided to start a blog! I'm not too sure what I'll encounter during my journey, but I'll be sure to upload any cool things I come across so that all my viewers can share my experiences :)

I'm going to be sharing things like this cool selfie that I took!
I'm still kind of bummed out that this mission is projected to take two entire months. I know that's extremely fast compared to NASA's missions, but I have to admit that I expected much better from Orbital Weapons' state of the art technology. Two months is a long time to be alone. Sometimes I can't help but wonder if Riki, Saul, and Chet were looking for an opportunity to get rid of me...

Well, that's probably just my insecurities talking! I'm proud that they entrusted me with this mission. I can't wait to make it to Jupiter!
