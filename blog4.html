The king of Jupiter.

I have finally finished my Journey to Jupiter! I should be proud of that achievment, but I just feel very confused. I thought I would be the sole person to travel so far within the galaxy, but it turns out that someone had reached Jupiter before me! To top it off, this person wasn't even wearing a spacesuit! None of it made sense! Here's a picture so you know what I'm talking about.



I was a bit nervous about approaching him at first, but he turned out to be a pretty swell guy! He introduced himself to me as "The King of Jupiter." Bit of a pretentious title if you ask me, but someone who can casually withstand 2.5 Gs of force wearing just a hoody gets a pass from me.
When I asked The King of Jupiter if he knew where I could obtain materials for our Unalive Ball project, he refused to give me an answer. Instead, he told me, "The password to the truth is 'password'."

It didn't mean much to me. When I got back to my ship, I was able to use that password to unzip an old archive I had, but I remember not seeing anything noteworthy. I don't remember where I left the zip file, but it should be in my files somewhere.

Overall, this final encounter was very anticlimactic, but at least I got a wacky story out of it.

Forever in your heart,
Jory Saltman
